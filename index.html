About Us

Alkura Capital is a San Diego-based acquisition company focused on essential, people-driven service businesses. We are entrepreneurs and operators, not financial engineers. When we acquire a business, we are committed to its people, its customers, and its continued growth — for the long term.

The name Alkura is built from two ideas. Alki is the motto of Washington State, meaning "hope for the future." Eureka is the motto of California, meaning "I have found it." Together they form Alkura — the moment of discovery, and the belief in what comes next. That is how we approach every acquisition: we look for businesses that are already great, and we commit to making their future even stronger.

What We Look For

We acquire established, profitable service businesses — primarily in Southern California, with select remote or national businesses where the model supports it. Our focus is on companies with strong teams, transferable customer relationships, and a track record of consistent performance.

We hold businesses for the long term. We are not flipping, rolling up for a private equity exit, or optimizing for a short-term return. Retiring owners deserve a buyer who respects their legacy — we take the handoff seriously and invest in making businesses stronger, not just larger.
